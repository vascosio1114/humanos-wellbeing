# HumanOS Wellbeing

HumanOS is an AI wellbeing intelligence platform for schools, universities, hotels, organizations, and high-pressure teams.

It turns private daily wellbeing signals such as stress, sleep, focus, workload, motivation, vitality, and mood pressure into:

- personal support plans
- research-informed wellbeing profiles
- anonymous institutional insights
- early support recommendations
- wellbeing campaign intelligence

HumanOS is not a medical diagnosis tool. It does not provide clinical diagnosis, treatment, crisis intervention, or replacement for professional care.

## Product Positioning

HumanOS is a Wellbeing Intelligence Layer.

- CRM manages customers.
- ERP manages company operations.
- HumanOS manages wellbeing intelligence.

The long-term vision is to become the Operating System for Human Wellbeing.

## Safety Boundary

HumanOS provides general wellbeing awareness, early-support guidance, anonymous trend insights, support recommendations, and referral guidance only.

It does not diagnose, treat, predict, or prevent mental illness. It is not a medical device and is not a replacement for licensed professionals or existing school/workplace support channels.
